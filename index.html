Ledbetter's World Story!

Founders

The founders of the Ledbetter family as noted above are Chris and Kate. They were married in Phoenixville, PA in Sept. 2006 at the Columbia Train Station in front of God, friends and family. It is with this strong tradition of love and faith in each other that Chris and Kate have set forth to make the best family possible. With two great kids, Sidnie and Vaughan, they are continuing this tradition and are thought of founding members of the Ledbetter clan.
